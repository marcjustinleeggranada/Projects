### What is AI in chatbots?

AI-powered chatbots use several specialized branches of artificial intelligence to understand and converse naturally with humans. The key technologies that make this possible are Natural Language Processing to interpret what users are asking, Generative AI to construct context-aware answers, and speech recognition to capture spoken language.

### How does Amazon Lex simplify chatbot development?

Building chatbots traditionally requires specialized expertise in machine learning and weeks of complex coding to manage conversation flows. Amazon Lex simplifies this by automatically handling tasks like Natural Language Processing, speech parsing, and infrastructure scaling, so you can build functional bots in hours using simple configurations.
